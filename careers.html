Careers

All that talent, all that ambition – Boss Burgers is somewhere you can really bring it, every single day. We’re a business that’s 100% built on the energy and hunger of its people. Whether you join us in a restaurant or in a corporate role, you’ll never be short of opportunities to show what you’ve got. And if we like what we see, there’s no limit to how far you could go here.

Make a great career move today.

At BB, we want you to achieve and become the best possible version of you. Because the better you are, the better we are!

The world's your Hawaiian™ when you work at BB. It can be the start of a long and rewarding career as you develop and progress into restaurant management roles, or it can be a job that pays the bills, gives you some security and provides you with flexible working hours to fit around your family or study commitments.

Whatever your aspirations, a job at BB means guaranteed hours, free meals on your shift, a fun team to work with and training and development opportunities. You'll also be part of the cool things we do, like sponsoring Joseph Parker and CanTeen.
